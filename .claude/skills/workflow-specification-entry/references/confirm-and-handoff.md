## Verb Rule

The verb rides on the flow's context ‚Äî the selected `ACTIONS` entry's `verb`, or the DATA `verb` on the single-discussion path:

- **"Creating"** ‚Äî no spec exists (proposed grouping or fresh single discussion)
- **"Continuing"** ‚Äî spec is `in-progress`, or `completed` with pending sources
- **"Refining"** ‚Äî spec is `completed` with all sources extracted
