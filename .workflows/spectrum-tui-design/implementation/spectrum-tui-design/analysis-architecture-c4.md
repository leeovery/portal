  SEVERITY: medium
  FILES: internal/tui/modal.go:32, internal/tui/modal.go:67, internal/tui/modal.go:21-40, internal/tui/modal.go:104-150
  DESCRIPTION: The reskin migrated all five modals (kill, rename, delete, edit, help) onto hand-drawn `renderJoinedPanel` content builders, each routed through its own `render*ModalOnClearedCanvas` wrapper from the two page composers (model.go:4064-4082 Sessions, model.go:3872-3890 Projects). If a future modal is genuinely expected to reuse a lipgloss auto-border box, keep `modalBorderStyle` but rewrite its doc to say it is currently unused and is a starting point — do not claim a non-existent set of consumers. Either way, scrub the "not-yet-reskinned modals" framing from the five wrapper comments.
